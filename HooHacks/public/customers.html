Order Accepted!


We will reach out to you once your order is being processed and on its way to your door, as we promote social distancing through non-contact delivery. Payments will be made directly to the volunteer driver through PayPal or Venmo, unless other arrangements have been made.

Make sure to take out your groceries on a clean table and to transfer items from their respective plastic/cardboard packaging to different containers if possible.
