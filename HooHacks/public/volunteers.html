Follow these sanitizing tips!

  • Go to the grocery store when it is not crowded
  • Use sanitizing wipes on cart and products
  • Wear gloves and properly dispose of them when done
  • Use wipes to open freezer doors
  • Don't touch face while shopping
  • Social distancing (6 ft)
  • Try paying virtually at cashier by tapping card or Apple Pay/Android Pay
  • Sanitize before touching door handle
  • Wash fresh produce and use disinfectant spray on cardboard/plastic items
  • Clean reusable bag if that is what you are using
